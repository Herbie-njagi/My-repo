## Summary

Nathan Gotch discusses how the meaning of SEO is evolving with the rise of AI-powered search. He suggests that **AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)** better reflects today's search landscape because it includes both optimizing brand visibility in AI-generated answers and ensuring that AI agents can easily understand and interpret websites. While he also mentions "Search Everywhere Optimization" as another possible term, he believes AEO is more likely to become the industry standard.

## Key Takeaways

- AEO extends traditional SEO to include AI-powered search experiences.
- Answer Engine Optimization focuses on increasing brand visibility in AI-generated responses.
- Agentic Engine Optimization helps AI agents better understand websites and brands.
- Businesses should optimize content for both human users and AI systems.
- AI search is expanding the role of SEO beyond traditional search engine rankings.

## Short Quote

> "AEO is a strong replacement for SEO."

## Why It's Relevant

This post is relevant because it explains how AI-powered SEO content production is evolving beyond traditional search optimization. As AI assistants and answer engines become more common, marketers need to create content and websites that are easily understood, trusted, and referenced by both search engines and AI systems.
