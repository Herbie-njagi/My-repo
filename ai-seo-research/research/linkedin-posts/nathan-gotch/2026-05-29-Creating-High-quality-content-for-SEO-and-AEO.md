## Summary

Nathan Gotch outlines a structured framework for creating content that performs well in both traditional SEO and Answer Engine Optimization (AEO). He emphasizes that AI alone does not create high-quality content; success comes from combining search data, company knowledge, expert insights, competitor analysis, and unique positioning. He recommends building comprehensive content briefs before writing so that AI or human writers can produce differentiated, valuable content that stands out in both search engines and AI-generated results.

## Key Takeaways

- Combine search performance data, business knowledge, and expert insights before creating content.
- Analyze top-performing competitors to identify successful content structures and important entities.
- Perform page-level gap analyses to discover missing topics and opportunities.
- Develop a unique content angle ("purple cow") that differentiates your content from competitors.
- Build detailed content briefs before using AI or human writers to ensure higher-quality output.
