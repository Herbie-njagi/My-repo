# Search Everywhere Optimization

Author: Nathan Gotch  
Date: 2026-05-22

Post URL: 
https://www.linkedin.com/posts/nathangotch_ive-been-seo-for-15-years-crazy-heres-share-7461381039605694464-Bc2R/?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAE2leM0BatNg6H_5FObReUU19gunleKlMeU

## Summary

Nathan Gotch reflects on his 15 years of experience in SEO and argues that the industry should redefine SEO as **Search Everywhere Optimization**. Instead of focusing only on Google rankings, he explains that users now search across many platforms, including AI assistants, social media, e-commerce marketplaces, and app stores. The attached graphic illustrates this shift by showing that modern search spans more than 20 platforms, requiring businesses to optimize their presence wherever potential customers search.

## Key Takeaways

- Modern SEO extends beyond traditional search engines.
- Users discover brands through AI platforms, social media, marketplaces, and app stores.
- Businesses should optimize content for multiple search ecosystems instead of Google alone.
- AI search platforms such as ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google AI are becoming important discovery channels.
- A diversified search strategy improves brand visibility across the entire customer journey.

## Image Notes

The image presents the concept of **Search Everywhere Optimization**, grouping search platforms into five categories:

- **Traditional Search:** Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo, Brave
- **AI Platforms:** Google AI, ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Grok
- **E-commerce:** Amazon, eBay, Etsy
- **Social Platforms:** YouTube, TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest, Reddit
- **App Stores:** Apple App Store and Google Play

The graphic emphasizes that customers no longer rely on a single search engine and instead discover products and information across a wide range of digital platforms.

## Short Quote

> "Switch your definition of 'SEO' to Search Everywhere Optimization."

## Why It's Relevant

This post is highly relevant because it reflects the evolution of AI-powered SEO content production. Modern optimization is no longer limited to Google Search—it requires creating content that is discoverable across AI assistants, social media, video platforms, marketplaces, and other digital ecosystems where users search for information and products.
