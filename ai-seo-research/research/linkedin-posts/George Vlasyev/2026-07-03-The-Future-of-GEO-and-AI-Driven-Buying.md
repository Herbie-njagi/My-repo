## Summary

George Vlasyev shares key insights from his podcast episode with Alp Aysan, Co-Founder of Cognizo, discussing how conversational AI and autonomous agents are transforming the customer buying journey. He argues that businesses must move beyond optimizing for website clicks and instead focus on Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) to ensure AI assistants can discover, evaluate, and recommend their products. The discussion highlights how AI search differs from traditional SEO and why brand visibility in AI-generated responses will become increasingly important.

## Key Takeaways

- Conversational AI assistants are changing how consumers make purchasing decisions.
- Businesses need to optimize for AI visibility, not just traditional search rankings.
- AI-generated recommendations are probabilistic and can vary depending on model behavior.
- Long-form, information-rich content is more likely to earn AI citations than thin or shallow content.
- Future digital marketing will focus on producing verifiable outcomes rather than simply driving website traffic.

## Short Quote

> "Stop optimizing purely for human website clicks."

## Why It's Relevant

This post is highly relevant because it explains why AI-powered SEO content production is evolving into Generative Engine Optimization (GEO). As AI assistants increasingly influence purchasing decisions, businesses must create authoritative, well-structured content that AI systems can easily understand, trust, and cite, rather than relying solely on traditional search engine optimization.
