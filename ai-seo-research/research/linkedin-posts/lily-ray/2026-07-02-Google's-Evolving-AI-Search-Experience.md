## Summary

Lily Ray comments on Google's latest announcement regarding AI search features, suggesting it supports the idea that AI Mode will eventually evolve into a more integrated search experience rather than a standalone AI interface. She expects future AI search results to include a richer mix of traditional website links, creator content, and user-generated content (UGC). She also praises Google's new improvements for recipe websites and recognizes the efforts of industry advocates who pushed for better support of creators.

## Key Takeaways

- Google's AI search experience is expected to become more integrated with traditional search results.
- Future AI search interfaces may include more links to websites, creators, and user-generated content.
- Recent Google updates provide better visibility for recipe websites.
- Supporting creators and high-quality websites remains an important part of AI search development.
- Businesses should continue investing in authoritative content rather than relying solely on AI-focused tactics.
