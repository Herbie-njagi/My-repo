## Summary

Cyrus Shepard discusses the relationship between traditional search engine optimization (SEO) and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO). While he agrees that many SEO best practices also benefit AI search, he argues that optimizing for AI requires additional strategies that go beyond conventional SEO. He highlights several techniques that have become increasingly important with the rise of AI-powered search, emphasizing that marketers should adapt to these new search environments instead of relying solely on Google's guidance.

## Key Takeaways

- Traditional SEO and AI search optimization overlap but are not identical.
- AI visibility should be monitored alongside traditional search performance.
- Optimizing for AI includes improving entity consistency, explicit factual content, and AI crawler accessibility.
- Fan-out query optimization and publishing transcript-based content can increase AI visibility.
- SEO professionals should adapt to evolving AI search platforms rather than relying only on conventional SEO advice.
