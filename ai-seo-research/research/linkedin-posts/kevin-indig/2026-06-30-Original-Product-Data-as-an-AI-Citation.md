## Summary

Kevin Indig explains that one of the strongest ways to earn AI citations is by publishing original data generated from a company's own product. Because competitors cannot replicate proprietary data, it creates a unique competitive advantage. However, he emphasizes that original research alone is not enough—content must also be structured for easy extraction and published on a domain that AI systems already recognize as trustworthy.

## Key Takeaways

- Proprietary product data is a valuable source of unique content for AI search.
- Original research alone does not guarantee AI citations.
- AI systems prefer content that presents important data in a clear, structured format.
- Domain authority and topical trust influence whether AI models choose to cite a source.
- Combining original data, clear formatting, and strong authority improves AI visibility.

## Short Quote

> "Owning the number may not win you the citation."

## Why It's Relevant

This post is relevant because it demonstrates that successful AI-powered SEO content production requires more than creating original research. Businesses should publish proprietary insights, organize them in a way that AI systems can easily extract, and build long-term authority so their content becomes a trusted source for AI-generated search results.
