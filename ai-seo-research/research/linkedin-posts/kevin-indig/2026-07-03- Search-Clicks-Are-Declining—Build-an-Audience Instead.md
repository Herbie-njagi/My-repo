## Summary

Kevin Indig highlights the ongoing decline in organic search clicks and argues that businesses should focus on building and nurturing their own audiences rather than relying solely on search traffic. As AI-generated search results answer more user questions directly, attracting loyal subscribers, customers, and community members becomes increasingly important for sustainable growth.

## Key Takeaways

- Organic search clicks are becoming less reliable as AI search evolves.
- Businesses should prioritize audience building alongside SEO.
- Strong relationships with customers reduce dependence on search engines.
- Email newsletters, communities, and social media can help retain audience attention.
- Long-term marketing success depends on owning your audience, not just earning clicks.

## Short Quote

> "Search clicks are fading."

## Why It's Relevant

This post is relevant because it reflects a major shift in AI-powered SEO content production. As AI search reduces website clicks, marketers need to create valuable content that builds trust, encourages repeat engagement, and develops owned audiences through channels such as newsletters, communities, and social media.
