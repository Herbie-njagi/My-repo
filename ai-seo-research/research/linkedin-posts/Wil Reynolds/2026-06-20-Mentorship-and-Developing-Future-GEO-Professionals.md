## Summary

Wil Reynolds reflects on the rewarding experience of mentoring interns and watching them develop their skills in Generative Engine Optimization (GEO). He recognizes several Seer Interactive team members for helping train interns through hands-on experiments, portfolio building, and professional development. Reynolds also explains that expanding the internship program reflects Seer's commitment to creating opportunities for young professionals entering the AI and search marketing industry.

## Key Takeaways

- Mentorship plays an important role in developing future SEO and GEO professionals.
- Practical experience and real-world experiments help interns build valuable skills.
- Technical training should be combined with professional and communication skills.
- Companies have a responsibility to create learning opportunities as AI changes the workforce.
- Investing in new talent strengthens both the organization and the marketing industry.

## Short Quote

> "It was so rewarding to watch someone 'get it.'"

## Why It's Relevant

This post is relevant because it highlights the importance of developing human expertise alongside AI technologies. While AI-powered SEO tools continue to evolve, successful AI SEO still depends on skilled professionals who understand strategy, experimentation, and critical thinking. Mentorship and practical training remain essential for preparing marketers to work effectively with AI.
