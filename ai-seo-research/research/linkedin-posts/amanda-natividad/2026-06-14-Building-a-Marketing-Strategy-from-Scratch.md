## Summary

Amanda Natividad explains that an effective marketing strategy should begin by understanding how a business is designed to grow. Rather than applying the same tactics to every company, she recommends identifying whether the business follows a discovery-led, product-led, or sales-led growth model. Once the growth mechanism is clear, marketers can prioritize content and initiatives that directly support that model and quickly demonstrate marketing's value to the organization.

## Key Takeaways

- A marketing strategy should align with the company's primary growth model.
- Discovery-led businesses benefit from SEO, educational content, and comparison pages.
- Product-led companies should prioritize onboarding resources, templates, and lifecycle content.
- Sales-led organizations need marketing assets that help sales teams educate and convert prospects.
- Early marketing efforts should focus on achieving measurable results to build trust within the company.
