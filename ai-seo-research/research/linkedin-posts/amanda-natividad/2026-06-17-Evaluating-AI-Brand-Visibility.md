## Summary

Amanda Natividad explains how marketers can evaluate their brand's visibility in AI-powered search using HubSpot's AEO Grader. Using SparkToro as an example, she demonstrates how the tool can reveal how AI categorizes a brand, identify strengths and misconceptions, and uncover opportunities to improve website content, messaging, public relations, and sales enablement.

## Key Takeaways

- AI visibility reports can help identify how AI platforms understand and describe a brand.
- Comparing AI-generated competitor lists can reveal misconceptions about a company's market position.
- Share of Voice metrics vary across AI platforms such as ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity.
- Incorrect AI assumptions should be addressed through improved website copy, content strategy, and brand messaging.
- AI visibility should be monitored regularly to measure improvements over time.
