## Featured Examples

### Manifesto Library at Livraria Lello
A literary initiative connected book censorship, freedom of expression, and cultural discussion through a permanent collection of banned and censored books. The campaign gained attention by linking an influential public figure with a socially relevant topic.

### Louis Vuitton Waterfall Installation
A fashion event generated discussion by creating a highly visible installation during an extreme weather event. The campaign demonstrated how brands can attract attention through spectacle, while also highlighting the reputational risks that can accompany controversial executions.

### Starbucks Bearista Cup Relaunch
Starbucks leveraged a previous product success by introducing a limited-release collectible item with restricted availability. The strategy created urgency and encouraged consumer demand through exclusivity.
