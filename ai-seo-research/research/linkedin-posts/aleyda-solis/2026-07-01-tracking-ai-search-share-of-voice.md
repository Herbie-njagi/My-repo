## Summary

Aleyda Solís recommends FreeSOV, a tool created by Ryan Jones for tracking a brand's visibility across AI search platforms. She highlights that the tool offers an affordable way to measure AI Search Share of Voice while allowing users to provide their own API keys, making it accessible for marketers and SEO professionals monitoring AI search performance.

## Key Takeaways

- FreeSOV measures a brand's AI Search Share of Voice over time.
- The tool tracks citations, brand mentions, and competitor visibility.
- Users can analyze AI query fan-out to understand related search topics.
- Sentiment analysis helps evaluate whether AI responses portray a brand positively, neutrally, or negatively.
- Users can monitor API costs associated with different AI providers.
