## Summary

Aleyda Solís shares and recommends Cyrus Shepard's **Fan-out Framework**, a practical five-step process for improving both traditional SEO performance and AI search visibility. The framework focuses on expanding existing high-ranking content by identifying the additional questions and subtopics that AI search engines generate during fan-out queries. By optimizing content around these related topics and measuring the results, marketers can increase their chances of appearing in both search engine rankings and AI-generated responses.

## Key Takeaways

- Start with keyword topics where your website already ranks well.
- Identify AI fan-out queries related to those topics.
- Prioritize the most valuable fan-out topics based on relevance and search potential.
- Update existing content to address these additional user intents.
- Measure the impact of the optimizations on both SEO performance and AI visibility.
