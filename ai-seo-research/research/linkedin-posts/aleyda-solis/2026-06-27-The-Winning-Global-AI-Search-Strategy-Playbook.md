## Summary

In this post, Aleyda Solís introduces her presentation, **"The Winning Global AI Search Strategy Playbook,"** which explains how businesses should adapt their SEO strategies for AI-powered search platforms. She discusses the evolution of search behavior, the importance of measuring AI-related business metrics, and aligning optimization efforts with AI search performance.

The accompanying presentation introduces a framework for evaluating AI readiness by examining factors such as content accessibility, usefulness, credibility, consistency, freshness, and how easily AI systems can understand and reference a brand's information.

## Key Takeaways

- Search strategies should evolve to account for AI-powered search engines and assistants.
- Businesses should track AI-specific visibility and performance metrics alongside traditional SEO metrics.
- AI-ready content should be accessible, trustworthy, well-structured, and regularly updated.
- Consistent entity information across trusted sources improves AI recognition.
- Clear product information, pricing, and unique positioning help AI systems generate accurate recommendations.
