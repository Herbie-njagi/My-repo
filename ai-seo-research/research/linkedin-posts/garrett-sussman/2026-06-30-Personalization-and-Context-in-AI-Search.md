## Summary

Garrett Sussman shares key ideas from his SEO Week presentation, explaining that AI-powered search is becoming increasingly personalized. He argues that AI systems now consider much more than a user's search query, including explicit preferences, previous interactions, platform memory, and personal signals. Because AI search platforms use this additional context to generate responses, marketers need to rethink how they measure visibility and understand different audience behaviors.

## Key Takeaways

- AI search results are influenced by explicit user preferences and hidden contextual signals.
- Platform memory and personal information can affect recommendations and search responses.
- Traditional rank tracking is becoming less reliable as AI search becomes more personalized.
- Audience research and user personas are essential for understanding how different users experience AI search.
- Marketers should develop new methods for measuring AI visibility in personalized search environments.
