# Collection Notes

## Core Ideas

### SEO is the Foundation of AI Search

AI Search does not replace SEO.

Strong traditional SEO increases the likelihood of appearing in:
- ChatGPT
- Google AI Overviews
- Perplexity
- Claude
- Gemini

---

### Search Everywhere Optimization

Optimize your entire digital presence, not just your website.

Include:
- Website
- YouTube
- LinkedIn
- Podcasts
- PR
- Reddit
- Industry publications

---

### AI Uses Retrieval

Large Language Models retrieve information from many trusted sources.

Your website is only one source.

---

### Brand Mentions > Website Mentions

Repeated mentions across trusted websites build AI confidence.

Focus on:
- PR
- Interviews
- Guest posts
- Reviews
- Industry citations

---

### AI Rewards Authority

Authority comes from:

- Expertise
- Experience
- Trust
- Brand recognition
- Consistent mentions
