# Ready-to-Paste Article

Artificial Intelligence is changing SEO, but the biggest opportunity is
building genuine authority rather than chasing AI optimization tricks.

Brands that earn trust through independent reviews, awards, media
mentions, and expert recommendations are more likely to be cited by AI
systems than brands relying on self-promotion.

Technical SEO remains essential because AI must be able to crawl,
understand, and interpret your content effectively.
