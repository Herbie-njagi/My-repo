## Topic
SEO / Product-Led SEO / AI Search / Business Strategy

## Summary

In this interview on the Ahrefs Podcast, Eli Schwartz explains why the future of SEO lies in building products that genuinely solve user problems rather than simply optimizing content for keywords. He introduces the concept of **Product-Led SEO**, where search strategy begins with understanding user intent and creating valuable experiences that naturally generate search demand. Instead of chasing rankings with thousands of pages, businesses should focus on creating products, tools, and resources that users actually want.

Eli also discusses his experience consulting for enterprise companies, sharing insights on pricing, working with large organizations, and how SEO should influence product development rather than exist as a standalone marketing function. He argues that while AI is changing how people search, companies with useful products, strong brands, and authentic expertise will continue to succeed because AI increasingly rewards genuine value over search engine manipulation.

## Key Takeaways

- Product-Led SEO starts with solving customer problems, not targeting keywords.
- SEO should influence product strategy, user experience, and business decisions—not just content creation.
- Programmatic SEO is a scaling method, not a strategy, and only works when it provides real value.
- Enterprise SEO success depends more on implementation and organizational alignment than technical recommendations.
- Content should support the entire customer journey instead of focusing only on high-volume keywords.
- Businesses with strong products and trusted brands are better positioned to succeed in AI-powered search.
- AI is changing search behavior, but creating genuinely useful products remains the most sustainable SEO strategy.
- SEO consultants should charge based on the business value they create rather than hourly rates.
