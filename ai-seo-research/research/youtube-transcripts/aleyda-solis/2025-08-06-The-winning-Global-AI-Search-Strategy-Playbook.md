# The Winning Global AI Search Strategy Playbook

**Video URL:** https://youtu.be/e4nG7DbOoaM?si=Wu45fSY5cTkqscqL

**Published:** 2025-08-06

## Topic
AI Search / GEO / SEO / AI Visibility Strategy

## Summary

In this presentation, Aleyda Solís explains how AI-powered search is reshaping the search landscape and why businesses need to evolve beyond traditional SEO. She discusses the growing influence of AI search platforms on user behavior and emphasizes that marketers should measure AI visibility alongside conventional search performance to understand their overall search presence.

The presentation also outlines practical strategies for improving visibility in AI-generated results. Aleyda highlights the importance of creating authoritative, user-focused content, aligning optimization efforts with AI-specific metrics, and connecting SEO activities with measurable business outcomes. Her overall message is that AI search optimization builds upon strong SEO fundamentals rather than replacing them.

## Key Takeaways

- AI search is changing how users discover information, requiring businesses to adapt their search strategies.
- AI visibility should be monitored alongside traditional SEO metrics to gain a complete view of search performance.
- Creating authoritative, high-quality content improves visibility in both search engines and AI-generated answers.
- AI-related metrics should be tied to business goals to evaluate the impact of optimization efforts.
- Successful AI search strategies build on strong SEO foundations instead of treating GEO and SEO as separate disciplines.
