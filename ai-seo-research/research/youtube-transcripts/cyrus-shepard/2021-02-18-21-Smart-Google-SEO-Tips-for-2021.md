# 21 Smart Google SEO Tips for 2021

**Video URL:** https://youtu.be/dwXa7HmOn4k?si=OX4A948KBnKDQnvG 
**Published:** 2021-02-18

## Topic
SEO / Content Strategy / Technical SEO / Link Building

## Summary
In this Whiteboard Friday episode, Cyrus Shepard shares 21 practical SEO strategies that can improve organic search performance without relying solely on higher rankings. He explains that many SEO gains come from increasing click-through rates, improving user experience, refreshing existing content, strengthening internal linking, and making websites easier for search engines to crawl and understand.

The video covers four major areas: earning more clicks from existing rankings, improving content quality and site architecture, optimizing technical SEO, and building sustainable backlinks. Rather than chasing shortcuts, Shepard encourages marketers to focus on creating better experiences for users while ensuring their websites are technically sound and highly discoverable. His central message is that the best SEO strategy is to satisfy user intent completely, becoming the "last click" users need before finding their answer.

## Key Takeaways

### Increase Click-Through Rate (CTR)
- Optimize your favicon since Google displays it in mobile search results and it can improve visibility.
- Use keyword-rich breadcrumbs so search results are clearer and more compelling.
- Write unique, persuasive meta descriptions even though Google sometimes rewrites them.
- Test adding dates or numbers to title tags when relevant to improve CTR.
- Audit repeated title tag boilerplate to ensure every page has a unique and valuable title.
- Implement FAQ and How-To schema where appropriate to gain more search result real estate.

### Improve Content & On-Page SEO
- Refresh and relaunch older high-performing content instead of always creating new articles.
- Increase internal linking to distribute authority across important pages.
- Add links from older articles to newly published content.
- Remove unnecessary internal links that dilute link equity.
- Ensure mobile pages contain the same important links as desktop pages.
- Invest in comprehensive, long-form content that naturally earns more links and shares.
- Use descriptive H2 and H3 headings to improve readability and increase featured snippet opportunities.
- Build topic clusters by creating multiple related articles that link to one another.
- Move important content out of tabs or accordions whenever possible to encourage better engagement.

### Strengthen Technical SEO
- Improve Core Web Vitals and page experience metrics, including loading speed and visual stability.
- Keep XML sitemaps organized and consider using smaller sitemaps for large websites to improve crawling efficiency.
- Use dynamic sitemaps to prioritize Google's crawling of your highest-value pages.

### Build Better Links
- Create content that naturally attracts backlinks without requiring constant outreach.
- Use page-level competitor link analysis to identify websites most likely to link to your content.

### Focus on User Satisfaction
- Aim to become the "last click" by fully answering the user's query and eliminating the need to return to search results.
- The strongest long-term SEO strategy is delivering the best experience for users, not simply ranking higher.
