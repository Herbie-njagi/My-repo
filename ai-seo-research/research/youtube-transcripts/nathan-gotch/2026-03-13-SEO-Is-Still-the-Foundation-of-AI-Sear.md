## Executive Summary

One of the biggest misconceptions in AI Search is the belief that traditional SEO no longer matters.

The reality is the opposite:

> There is no successful AI Search Optimization (AEO/GEO) without strong SEO.

However, SEO has evolved.

SEO is no longer just about optimizing your website. Today's search landscape requires **Search Everywhere Optimization**, where AI evaluates your entire digital presence—not just your domain.

The strongest AI brands dominate across:

- Traditional search
- Industry publications
- PR
- Reviews
- Brand mentions
- YouTube
- Authority websites
- Trusted resources
- AI citation sources

Your website is only one piece of your overall authority.

---

# The Big Idea

### Old SEO

Optimize your website.

### AI Search

Optimize your entire online presence.

AI systems retrieve information from dozens of trusted sources before generating answers.

If your brand consistently appears across those sources, AI is far more likely to recommend you.

---

# Why SEO Still Matters

The speaker challenged LinkedIn with the statement:

> "I'm still waiting to see a brand that dominates AI Search but doesn't perform well in traditional search."

After analyzing many examples, the conclusion remained the same:

- SEO remains one of the strongest ranking signals for AI answers.
- AI search is built on search engine signals.
- GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) extends SEO—it doesn't replace it.
